Here's What You'll Need

  1. 3 Tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  2. 2 Pints of cherry tomatos
  3. 1 Brick o feta
  4. 2 Cloves of garlic
  5. 1 Teaspoon dried basil

Steps

This is the best part! It's so easy. Just heat that oven up to 425 degress
Yolo all your ingrediants together and in a baking dish and put it in your
preheated oven. Bake for 50 mins or until the feta is golden brown. Mix together
and put on pasta or toasted bread bites. Yum.
